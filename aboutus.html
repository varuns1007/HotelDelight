About Us

Our History

Started in 2019, Hotel Delight quickly established itself as a dessert icon par excellence in India. With its unique brand of world fusion desserts that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the various parts of the world. Featuring four of the best three-star Michelin chefs in the world, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to Sweet Hunger , a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Varun Shinde, that featured for the first time as the world's best dessert.
